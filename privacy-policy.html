S.I.R. Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 7, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how S.I.R. (Search & Intelligence Research) collects, uses, and stores data when you interact with the bot on Discord. By using S.I.R., you agree to this policy.

2. Data We Collect

When you use S.I.R., we may collect the following information:

  • Discord User ID & Username: Used to track research history and attribute document uploads.
  • Server (Guild) ID: Used to scope documents and history to the correct server.
  • Channel ID: Used to deliver responses to the correct channel.
  • Research Queries: The questions you submit via /research are stored for history tracking.
  • Uploaded Document Content: Text extracted from files uploaded via /upload is stored for search and indexing purposes.
  • AI-Generated Answers: Responses generated in reply to your queries are stored alongside your query history.

3. How We Use Your Data

Data collected by S.I.R. is used exclusively to:

  • Provide AI-powered research answers from indexed documents.
  • Display your personal research history via /history.
  • List indexed documents available in your server via /documents.

We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising or any purpose outside of operating the bot.

4. Data Storage

All data is stored securely on the Base44 platform. Document content and query history are retained as long as the bot remains active on your server.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

You may request deletion of your personal data (query history, user ID references) by contacting the server administrator or bot owner. Admins may also request full removal of all server data at any time.

6. Third-Party Services

S.I.R. may use the following third-party services to operate:

  • Discord API: For receiving commands and sending responses. Subject to Discord's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenAI API: For generating AI-powered research answers. Query context (document excerpts + your question) may be sent to OpenAI. Subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • Base44: For data storage and backend hosting. Subject to Base44's Privacy Policy.

7. Children's Privacy

S.I.R. is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of S.I.R. after changes are made constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions or data deletion requests, please contact the server administrator or bot owner through your Discord server.
